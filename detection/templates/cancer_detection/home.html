Welcome to Cancer Detection AI

Project Overview

Our Cancer Detection AI project leverages cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning techniques to analyze medical images and assist in the early detection of cancer. By processing MRI and CT scan images, our system can identify potential malignancies with high accuracy, providing valuable insights to healthcare professionals.

Key Features
  • Advanced neural network architecture for image analysis
  • Rapid processing of medical images
  • High accuracy in detecting various types of cancer
  • Secure handling of sensitive medical data
  • User-friendly interface for medical professionals

Our AI model has been trained on a vast dataset of medical images, enabling it to recognize patterns and anomalies that may indicate the presence of cancer. While this tool is designed to augment the capabilities of healthcare professionals, it is not a substitute for expert medical diagnosis.
